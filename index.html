ADOPTIONDC BLUE MISSION

The DCAdoption Blue Animal Rescue Project is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization under the umbrella of Hands For Paws in Florida. We work alongside other animal rescue organizations in New York City and in Puerto Rico, such as the Humane Society, The Center for Animal Care and Control and Amigos de los Animales. DCAdoption Blue Animal Rescue is an organization committed to the empowerment of youth with a distinct focus on Foster children. Starting in Puerto Rico, we bridge the gap in the lack of education for Humane Behavior toward animals.

ANIMAL RESCUE

Working alongside other animal protection and rescue organizations in New York City and Puerto Rico, lnky Blue’s goal is to foster responsible and humane behaviors toward companion animals through education, communication, and funding of rescue programs.

YOUTH PROJECT

“Our purpose is to educate and introduce resources and tools for children to be better able to understand situations and use problem-solving tactics acquired from their newly guided exposure to animals.”
